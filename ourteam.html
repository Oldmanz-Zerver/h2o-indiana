Hope Leimbach
Hometown: Columbus, Ohio
What did you study in college? Athletic training
Fun Fact: My favorite food is grilled cheese and tomato soup
Why are you a part of H2O Church Indiana? Growing up, I knew a lot about God but didn’t fully understand that she could have a relationship with Him. I went to college at Ohio State, quickly started attending H2O Church and was drawn to the vulnerability of the girls in her small group. Throughout college, I experienced the freedom and grace found in being authentic about life's struggles and how being bold with your faith leads to life transformation. I graduated in 2017 and decided to join the staff team with H2O Church at Indiana University because I'm passionate about discipling women to be leaders with their faith and helping students find joy in the Lord in the midst of hard circumstances.
In my free time, I like to: Drink coffee, play volleyball, make up random dance moves, watch marvel movies and use puns as often as possible
Primary Role: North / Central Campus Community Group, Outreach coordinator
Email: Hope.Leimbach@h2oindiana.org
Kelsey Keesecker
Hometown: Melbourne, Florida
What did you study in college? Social Work
Fun Fact: I know every word to every Taylor Swift song ever produced.
Why are you a part of H2O Church Indiana? Jesus captured my heart while I was in college and has given me a desire to share Him with people who are in a similar season I was when he first caught my attention. The "college years" are often seen as a time to do whatever you want with no repercussions, but I believe that God is moving in the hearts of college students and leveraging this time in their lives for BIG life transformation and kingdom work. Basically, I just want to be a part of it in any way I can.
In my free time, I like to: Laugh as much as possible and repeatedly try and fail at picking up any and every new hobby.
