AS A COMMUNITY

THE HEARTBEAT OF H2O

While we love to worship and learn together on Sundays, we know that people grow lasting connections in smaller groups. Our H2O Community Groups create space to grow in deeper spiritual community with one another and are truly the heart-beat of our church. These community groups are a place to feel at home, ask questions, and grow lasting friendships. We have seen hundreds of new students encounter God and be transformed by Him. We believe He wants to do the same for you!

WHETHER YOU ARE JOINING US IN PERSON OR ONLINE, OUR HOPE IS THAT YOU'LL FEEL RIGHT AT HOME

From meeting new friends, finding your classes, not to mention the challenges that come with COVID-19, coming to college can definitely be an overwhelming experience. Whether you are feeling overwhelmed or are just excited to get started, we understand where you are at and want to walk alongside you in this journey.
