Fall Retreat

We are getting away from campus together as a church for 24 hours to be together and learn more about the heart of Jesus at our annual Fall Retreat! Join us for a weekend full of encouraging talks, worship through music, games, campfires, and nature. It is an excellent opportunity to build deeper friendships with others at H2O - you aren't going to want to miss it.

DATE

Friday, September 30 - Saturday, October 1

TIME

7p Friday - 5p Saturday

COST

$49 (covers lodging, 3 meals, and all activities)

LOCATION

Camp Indicoso (about 20 minutes south of Bloomington)

**Scholarships are available. If finances are an issue, please email or talk to Kevin Cody Kevin.Cody@H2OIndiana.org. We do not want finances to hinder you from attending.
