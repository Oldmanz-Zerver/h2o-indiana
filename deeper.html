Deeper

DEEPER IS A COMMUNITY THAT FOSTERS BEING DISCIPLES AND MAKING DISCIPLES THROUGH ENCOURAGING ONE ANOTHER, EXHORTING ONE ANOTHER, AND TEAMING UP WITH EACH OTHER.

Lead yourself to lead others! You're invited to join the discipleship community at H2O called Deeper.

It's designed to help you thrive spiritually and to help others grow closer to Jesus. This will propel us towards being a student led church. We are all created to go deeper with God, but we need help getting there! Let's gather to encourage and challenge one another. We will go through topics that focus on personally thriving spiritually because that's the life God meant for us. We will also discuss topics about helping others thrive spiritually because Jesus calls us to make disciples. We're going to lead ourselves in order to lead others!

Some of you have been a part of the Deeper community before, these training times are designed to help you grow whether you were a part of Deeper before or not!
