GIVE

Thank you for partnering with us to help Indiana University students become fully-devoted disciples of Jesus Christ.

All gifts made to H2O Church of Bloomington are tax deductible. We send a record of giving to all of our donors in January of the new year.

GIVE BY MAIL

If you wish to send a gift by mail simply make your gift out to ‘H2O Church of Bloomington’ and mail it to:

H2O Church of Bloomington
P.O. Box 7316
Bloomington, IN 47407

GIVE ONLINE

We are currently able to receive gifts online through ACH transactions (gifts directly from a bank account). This helps us to keep the fees for accepting gifts low.

Please note that it takes 5-7 business days for these gifts to process. After filling out the form below, you will receive an email receipt for your gift after the gift has been processed.

If you have any questions or concerns, please contact us at: info@h2oindiana.org .

Thank you for your support of H2O Church of Bloomington!
